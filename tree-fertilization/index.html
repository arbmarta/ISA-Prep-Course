Tree Nutrition and Fertilization

Complete Fertilizer Practice Questions

The guaranteed analysis on the bag of a complete fertilizer indicates the amounts of N-P-K. This activity helps you to practice the math used in determining fertilizer application amounts.

How to calculate each answer

Nutrient mass = bag mass × nutrient percentage ÷ 100

Worked example

A 20 kg bag of 10–6–3 fertilizer contains:

  • Nitrogen: 20 × 10 ÷ 100 = 2.0 kg
  • Available phosphate: 20 × 6 ÷ 100 = 1.2 kg
  • Soluble potash: 20 × 3 ÷ 100 = 0.6 kg
